When planning a trip, one of the biggest decisions is whether to fly or drive. Here are key factors to consider:

### Cost Comparison
- **Flying**: Ticket prices vary by season and demand
- **Driving**: Fuel costs + potential hotel stays

### Time Considerations
- Flying is faster for long distances
- Driving gives more flexibility

### Comfort Factors
- Airports vs. open road
- Luggage restrictions
