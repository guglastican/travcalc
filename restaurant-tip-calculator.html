How the Restaurant Tip Calculator Works

Whether you're dining out with friends at a new restaurant you found via our hotel map, enjoying a coffee, or paying for room service, knowing how much to tip should be quick and stress-free. Our Restaurant Tip Calculator is designed to do the math for you, calculating precise tip amounts and helping you split the bill effortlessly.

  • Accuracy: No more guessing or mental math. Get the exact tip and total bill amounts instantly.
  • Customization: Choose from standard percentages (10%, 15%, 20%, 25%) or enter a custom tip amount to suit the level of service you received.

Tipping Etiquette Tips for Travelers

Tipping customs vary by country and service type. In the United States, a 15% to 20% tip is standard for good service at sit-down restaurants. For exceptional service, 20% or more is a great way to show your appreciation.

When travelling internationally—perhaps taking a road trip using our Distance Calculator—be sure to research local tipping norms, as many countries include a service charge in the bill, requiring little to no additional tip. This is especially important when planning gap days with our Turnaround Calculator so you can budget accurately for your entire journey.

How to Budget for Dining Out While Traveling

Dining out is often one of the largest expenses on any trip. By using a tip calculator, you can accurately forecast the total cost of a meal before you even sit down. We recommend researching the average cost of meals in your destination city ahead of time and factoring in a standard 20% tip for budgeting purposes. This ensures you won't encounter any unpleasant surprises when the check arrives.
